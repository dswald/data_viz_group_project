If your company needed to choose a city for a second headquarters, how would you choose that city?
Amazon chose to prioritize:

large metropolitan areas
with a business-friendly environment,
good quality of life to attract and keep talent,
and direct flights to existing facilities,
and other factors.

HQ 2 City Finder is a data exploration tool to help choose a location for your company's new HQ.
HQ 2 City Finder was designed to support:

Users
  • Business executives
  • HR professionals
  • Frequent travelers
Tasks
  • Ranking candidate expansion cities
  • Exploring quality of life in those cities
  • Exploring airport city characteristics
Data
  • Travel: US Department of Transportation
  • Quality of life: Nerd Wallet
HQ 2 City Finder includes four visualizations:

  1. Air Travel, for exploring the availability of direct flights to from existing locations to the candidate cities.

  2. City Quality of Life, for exploring commute times, housing afforadability, unemployment, povery, health care access

  3. Air Migration, for understanding which cities are attracting new residents, and which are losing people.

  4. Air Efficiency, for understanding which airports do the best job in managing efficient air travel, as a proxy for limiting the environmental impact of air travel.

Technology Overview
We chose Tableau for interactive task support and filtering capabilities,

and Flourish for story-telling and high-quality graphics easily implemented.

We used Reveal.js for this tutorial for ease of use, quality of presentation, and multi-dimensional navigation.
